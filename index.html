FORTUNE FRENZY
High-Stakes Battle for Bountiful Rewards!
Step into "Fortune Frenzy," a tactical arena where survival could earn you the grand Game Pot. Ticket purchases amplify the Reserve Pool, adding a prize boost to each round. With unclaimed pots increasing future rewards, every skirmish is a gamble for a richer bounty. Triumph and seize your well-earned prize!
SKINS SKIRMISH
Unlock Exclusive Skins in Strategic Warfare!
The ultimate deathmatch where hunting players levels you up and unlocks exclusive weapon skins! As a free-to-play mode, not only do you earn these coveted skins, but you also become a vital supplier for the MetaHunt marketplace. Every level brings a chance for rare skins, but be wary: your XP is always at risk from opponents, adding a thrilling layer of strategy.
ARCADE MODE
CLASSIC DEATHMATCH
Relentless Combat, Pure Adrenaline! Dive into the chaos of the classic "Deathmatch," a raw, fast-paced battle where survival depends on quick reflexes and sharp shooting. With no allies, it’s every player for themselves in this timeless test of skill. Outlast and outgun your opponents to emerge victorious!
TEAM DEADTHMATCH
Team Up, Take Down, Triumph Together! Join forces in "Team Deathmatch," where collaboration is key. Work together with your teammates to outmaneuver and overpower rival squads. Shared objectives and team dynamics make every victory sweeter. Coordinate, combat, and conquer as a united front!
TOKEN TAKEDOWN
Stake, Fight, and Earn!
Stake your MHNT tokens and dive into "Token Takedown," a high-stakes deathmatch arena. Liminate opponents to seize their tokens, and aim to collect rewards up to your staked amount. With the clock ticking, strategy and combat skills are your keys to victory and richer rewards.

ABOUT OUR TOKEN
$MHNT
TOKENOMICS
Staking Rewards : 49.2% of total supply
COC Staking : 10.3% of total supply
Ecosystem & Partnerships : 13.5% of total supply
Development & Marketing : 8.5% of total supply
Team : 5% of total supply
Liquidity : 6% of total supply
Reserve : 7.5% of total supply
Embark on a strategic gaming journey with $MHNT, the lifeblood of the MetaHunt ecosystem. the on-chain token with off-chain in-game capabilities for a seamless experience, regardless of your preference for web3 elements. Think of it as the robustness of V-bucks, but with the solid backing of MHNT$.
Dive into the MetaHunt ecosystem where $MHNT fuels your strategic play and rewards your gaming prowess.
Tokenomics at a Glance:
-Total Supply: A carefully capped supply of 10 billion MHNT ensures value and utility. Rewarding Participation: With 49.2% dedicated to staking rewards, MHNT tokens incentivize player engagement and investment.
-Ecosystem Growth: Allocations for ecosystem and partnerships (13.5%) and development/marketing (8.5%) drive innovation and outreach.
-Liquidity and Stability: Reserved liquidity (6%) ensures smooth trading, while contingency funds (7.5%) safeguard the platform’s future.
Strategic Gameplay Mechanics:
-Mirrored Distribution: Earn passive income through token buys and sells, as a percentage is distributed to all holders, fostering a robust holding culture.
-Burn for Value: A progressive burn rate from transactions gradually reduces supply, enhancing the token’s scarcity and potential value over time.
Staking for Rewards:
-COC Staking: Rewarding COC token holders with a share of MHNT, promoting cross-platform token utility. In-game Staking: Players can stake MHNT for in-game advantages, aligning player success with direct token utility.
-Marketplace Dynamics: The in-game $MHNT marketplace facilitates trading with a 10% fee structure, contributing to platform sustainability and rewarding COC token stakers with converted ETH.
Engagement Amplified:
-Referral Bonuses: Grow the community with referral links, earning a percentage from ticket sales.
-Community Rewards: A portion of ticket sales is distributed to stakers, directly linking community support with tangible rewards.
