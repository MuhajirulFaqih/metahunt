What is metahunt
Welcome to MetaHunt, a high-stakes, web-based game that seamlessly blends survival, strategy, and rewarding gameplay.

Choose from various modes like Arcade Mode, Skins Skirmish, Token Takedown, and Fortune Frenzy, each offering unique challenges and prizes. Whether you're in it for the thrill of the hunt, the joy of collecting skins, or the ambition to earn cryptocurrency rewards, MetaHunt provides a competitive and exciting battlefield for every type of player.
